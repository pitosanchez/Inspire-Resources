ABOUT

Inspire Kidney Transplant Resources is committed to addressing the systemic challenges that hinder equitable access to kidney transplantation. We provide comprehensive educational tools, patient navigation support, and community-driven interventions designed to empower individuals from marginalized communities. Recognizing that structural racism, systemic barriers, and social determinants of health—including biases in provider decision-making, food and housing insecurity, and limited health literacy—contribute to disparities in transplant care, we have developed innovative strategies to break down these obstacles and ensure every individual has a fair chance at life-saving treatment.

In collaboration with trusted clinical and community partners, our initiatives focus on designing and testing interventions that mitigate the impact of unjust systems and policies. Our Community Health Worker (CHW) program plays a vital role in screening for barriers and connecting patients with essential resources—from transplant referrals and medical clearances to transportation and medication access. Through targeted patient education, structured documentation, and strategies to identify provider bias, we strive to create a more inclusive and equitable transplant process. Explore our resources to learn more about how we can support you on your kidney transplant journey.
